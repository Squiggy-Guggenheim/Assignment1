The LooSink Story

Once apon a time there was an man who thought to himself...'I think I would rather be using pen and paper than keyboard and computer screen.' He then went out and bought a pen and paper, many pens and much paper, and set about using these. He scribbled and scrawled all day and all night. The next day, he got up bright and early and again worked on his sribbles and scrawls. He worked day an night on his project until he was happy with the end product.

These amazing designs are the culmination of this hard work. They are now avaiable for you to purchase.
